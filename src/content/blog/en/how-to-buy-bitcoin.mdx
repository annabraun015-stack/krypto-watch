Before you buy, you must understand the infrastructure of the "on-ramp"—the gateway between the legacy banking system and the [Bitcoin Network](/en/blog/what-is-bitcoin/).

## Section 7: FAQ — Professional Troubleshooting

### 1. Can I buy Bitcoin without showing my ID?
Yes, using P2P (Peer-to-Peer) exchanges like Bisq or RoboSats. However, these platforms have a steeper learning curve and higher fees than regulated exchanges. They are used primarily by users who prioritize privacy over convenience.

### 2. Is my money safe if the exchange goes bankrupt?
In short: **Maybe not.** In 2026, many regulated exchanges have insurance, but the only way to be 100% safe is to follow the golden rule: *"Not your keys, not your coins."* Always withdraw to your own wallet.

### 3. Why are my fees so high?
Fees come from two places: the Exchange Fee (for the service) and the **Network Fee** (for the miners). To lower fees, use "Pro" interfaces with limit orders and withdraw during periods of low network activity (usually weekends).
