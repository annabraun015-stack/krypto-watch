## Section 7: FAQ (Beginner Pitfalls)

### 1. Is it too late to buy Bitcoin?
No. Bitcoin is designed to be a long-term store of value. As adoption moves from early speculators to global institutions and central banks, the network's value continues to mature.

### 2. Can the exchange steal my money?
If the exchange is unregulated or "offshore," yes. In 2026, sticking to BaFin-regulated or MiCA-compliant platforms reduces this risk, but the only 100% guarantee is withdrawing your BTC to **Self-Custody**.

### 3. What is 'SIM Swapping' and how do I prevent it?
SIM Swapping is when a hacker steals your phone number to bypass SMS codes. **Never use SMS for two-factor authentication.** Use an app like Google Authenticator or a physical YubiKey instead.
