## Experience & Background

Dr. Marcus Richter is a recognized expert in the intersection of traditional monetary theory and digital assets. Before joining KryptoWatch, he served as a Senior Economist at a major European central bank, where he first began his research into Decentralized Ledger Technology (DLT).

### Expertise
- **Macroeconomics**: Analyzing the impact of global interest rates and inflation on Bitcoin's stock-to-flow model.
- **Monetary Policy**: Comparing the historical evolution of gold as a reserve asset with Bitcoin's digital scarcity.
- **On-Chain Analysis**: Using network health metrics to identify long-term market cycles.

Marcus is a frequent speaker at European blockchain conferences and a regular contributor to peer-reviewed financial journals.
